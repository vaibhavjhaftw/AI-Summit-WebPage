What is Bengaluru AI Summit 2024?

The Bengaluru AI Summit 2024, held at The Lalit Ashok Hotel, is a premier event focused on the transformative impact of artificial intelligence. Organized by Elets Technomedia in partnership with the Government of Karnataka, this two-day summit features over 20 sessions with more than 100 thought leaders and 400 delegates. It aims to explore AI applications in governance, healthcare, education, and more, fostering collaboration and innovation to position India as a global leader in AI by 2047
